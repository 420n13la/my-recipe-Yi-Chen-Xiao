Grilled Cheese Recipe

What is a Grilled Cheese?

To put it simply, a grilled cheese is a sandwich with cheese between both bread slices.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread slices
  • Sliced cheese

Preperation

  1. Put some butter on the pan whilst on the stove
  2. Place two (2) slices of bread on the pan
  3. When both slices get a little darker, place cheese slices on them
  4. Flip both bread slices
  5. Combine both bread slices and cut the sanwich in a way to make two triangles
